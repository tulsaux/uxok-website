Frequently Asked Questions

  • Where is the conference? The Skyline on Bricktown Canal event venue. Located in downtown OKC, in the Bricktown neighborhood. It's above The Verge co-working space.
  • I’m new to UX. Is this conference for me? Absolutely. Our conference covers subjects for all UX knowledge levels.
  • Will I have a chance to network with other attendees? Yes! After the conference, we host an optional after-party for attendees to meet up.
  • Will food be provided during the conference? We will provide coffee, snacks, and drinks throughout the day, and lunch including vegan and gluten-free options (please mark any restrictions on your ticket registration form).
  • What's the difference in tickets? The corporate ticket is if your company is paying for attendance to the conference. You will help keep the conference affordable for everyone, especially students and those requiring a travel grant. Government employees should also register at the corporate rate. The general admission ticket is for individual attendees or non-profit/military/education attendees.

about

Founded in 2021, UXOK is a design conference that focuses on user experience and related tech that make the web accessible, delightful, and functional.

UXOK is organized and backed by the Techlahoma Foundation – a volunteer-run 501(c)3 non-profit. Techlahoma is dedicated to advancing Oklahomans through accessible tech training and community building.

See previous conference websites: 2022, 2021

Before attending the conference, please review the Techlahoma Code of Conduct.
