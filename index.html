UXOK is a design conference that focuses on user experience and related tech that make the web accessible, delightful, and functional.

UXOK is organized and backed by Techlahoma – a volunteer-run 501(c)3 non-profit. Techlahoma is dedicated to advancing Oklahomans through accessible tech training and community building.

FAQs

  • How much does it cost to attend?

    This conference is donation based, so it's pay-what-you-want.! If you donate a certain amount, we will send you a shirt and other UXOK goodies.

  • I’m new to UX. Is this conference for me?

    Absolutely. Our conference covers subjects for all UX knowledge levels.

  • Will I have a chance to network with other attendees?

    Yes! After the conference, we host a virtual after-party for attendees to meet. You'll need a (free) ticket to get the RSVP information for the after-party.

Before attending the conference, please review the Techlahoma Code of Conduct. The Code of Conduct applies to all attendees, speakers, volunteers, and vendors at official and unofficial events by Techlahoma and any location where attendees may be congregating. Contact us if you have questions.
